Analysis of 23 Years of USA Wildfires

Utilizing the Kaggle 188 Million Us Wildfires dataset I created two Tableu visualizations to show the steady growth in wildfires by acre in the USA. The dataset differs from the original in that I have only visualized the fires which are greater than 5000 acres in size for speed of query sake inside of the Tableu visualization.
